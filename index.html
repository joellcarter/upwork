A book that reads beautifully sells better.

"The difference between a manuscript and a book is not content. It's craft — the invisible architecture that makes a reader forget they're reading."

I'm Mir Shahid Ali, a book formatting specialist working with self-publishing authors and publishers worldwide. My work lives at the intersection of traditional typography and the technical requirements of modern platforms.

Every book I format is treated as what it actually is: a designed object. Margins are not defaults. Typefaces are chosen with intention. Chapter openings are crafted. Drop caps earn their place. Running heads are consistent. The result is a reading experience that doesn't draw attention to itself.

I work across Kindle Direct Publishing, IngramSpark, Apple Books, Kobo, and Barnes & Noble. My files don't generate KDP errors. They don't need corrections after upload. They look like books — not like Word documents renamed as PDFs.

The reader simply reads. That invisibility is the craft.

Process

How the work actually happens

From manuscript to market
01
You send the manuscript

Share your Word document, Google Doc, or any text file. Include style preferences — or let me make the decisions. I review the full document before work begins.

Word .docxGoogle DocsAny format
02
I build the style sheet

Before touching a single character, I establish the typographic system: typeface selection, type size, leading, margin ratios, chapter opening design, and hierarchy. This is where the book's visual identity is created.

TypographyMargin ratiosChapter design
03
Formatting & typesetting

The manuscript is flowed into the layout. Every widow and orphan is corrected. Hyphenation is checked. Images are placed correctly. Running heads are consistent. Page numbers fall where they should. I don't rush this.

Widow/orphan controlHyphenationImage placement
04
You review. We refine.

You receive a full PDF proof and, if applicable, an EPUB preview. Revisions are included until you're satisfied. I don't count revisions — if something isn't right, we fix it.

PDF proofEPUB previewUnlimited revisions
05
Delivery & upload support

You receive all files: print-ready PDF, EPUB3, and source files if requested. If you need help uploading to KDP or IngramSpark, I'm available to guide you through every step.
